BuyerFolio — Seller-side App Design

An AI-driven real estate platform redesign for sellers: guided onboarding, trust-first interactions, and AI-powered co-buyer matching to streamline listing and negotiations.

The Problem

Many sellers—especially elderly homeowners—struggle with fragmented tools, unclear progress, and overwhelming decisions. Existing buyer-focused flows caused confusion and drop-off when sellers tried to adapt them.

Need: A seller-dedicated experience that improves clarity, trust, and task success while integrating with existing app architecture.

Goals

  • Step-by-step onboarding for clarity and confidence.
  • AI-powered matching to enhance compatibility (Folio/Home Score).
  • Seller dashboard for transparent progress tracking.
  • Trust-first features: verification, agreements, clear microcopy.

Design Strategy

Human-centered, trust-driven, and AI-supported approach tailored for elderly homeowners and co-living arrangements.

  • Guided onboarding: Welcoming, progressive disclosure, collect property & personal info gradually.
  • AI matching: Folio Score and Home Score recommend compatible co-buyers to reduce friction.
  • Progress transparency: Centralized dashboard, clear prompts, large-touch UI elements.
  • Trust-first: Verified profiles, community agreements, transparent communication tools.
  • Modular tools: Offer rooms, messaging, scheduling within the platform.
  • Scalable design: Modular sections support legal templates, finance tools, neighborhood insights.

Solution Highlights

Onboarding & Trust

Clear steps, welcoming copy, and verified profiles increase confidence. Agreements and transparent policy microcopy reduce ambiguity.

AI Matching & Negotiation

Folio/Home scores drive better compatibility. Offer rooms, messaging, and scheduling keep conversations within the platform.
