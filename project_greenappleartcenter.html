Green Apple Art Center — Website & App UX Redesign

Public site, store, mobile app, and student portal redesigned to improve discoverability, storytelling, and weekly clarity.

Tools
Adobe Illustrator • Photoshop • Figma
Duration
3 months
My Role
Product Designer
Scope
Site IA • Filters • Store • Dashboard

About Project

The Green Apple Art Center specializes in visual arts education with programs across portfolio preparation, architectural design, and fashion management. The redesign focused on making program discovery fast, improving store storytelling, and clarifying weekly tasks for students.

Highlights

  • Integrated homepage filters: Student Level • Programs • Learning Mode.
  • Product story panel for persuasive content without layout clutter.
  • Student dashboard emphasizing weekly calendar and assignments.

Store — Product Storytelling

On the product page, a slide‑in story block adds narrative and specs without breaking the gallery alignment. Users can toggle it using the arrow, keeping key details in view.
